Registration Process

WeChat Official Account Setup

Setting up a WeChat Official Account as a foreign entity is complex. We manage the entire process — two clear pathways depending on your situation.

Option 1 · Recommended
You Have a Registered Company in China
Fastest path to full verification and feature access. Full ownership under your Chinese entity.
  1. Document Collection
    You provide business license, legal representative ID, corporate bank account, and certification documents.
  2. Registration & Verification
    We complete account registration and official WeChat verification, set up basic menus and functions.
  3. Permission Configuration
    We configure account access. Ownership remains with your company.
  4. Official Operation
    We deliver daily content, design, community interaction, and monthly data reports.
  5. Ongoing Support
    Regular content review, function adjustment, and annual certification renewal.
Option 2
No Chinese Company Yet
Two pathways available — standard overseas Service Account or quick-launch personal account.
  1. Standard: Register Overseas Service Account
    We collect your overseas company documents, contact person ID, and bank info. We submit and complete registration for the overseas corporate Service Account.
  2. Permission Setup
    We set up operation permissions. The account belongs to your overseas travel agency.
  3. Temporary: Personal Account Quick Launch
    We register a personal account for immediate content release and fan engagement during the transition.
  4. Migration When Ready
    Once you establish a Chinese company, we migrate all content and followers to your new corporate account.
