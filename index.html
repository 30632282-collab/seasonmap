The Opportunity

In China, a WeChat Official Account Is Your Best Brand Showcase

China's outbound travel market is massive — over 130 million travelers per year. With Seasonmap as your on-the-ground partner, the platforms, the content, and the leads all become simple and accessible.

01
Underestimated Platform Potential
Many overseas tourism boards and travel agencies have already registered WeChat Official Accounts — but their content is operated by under-invested or disconnected teams. The visual layout fails to reflect the beauty of the destination. Breathtaking landscapes and cultural treasures deserve world-class visual presentation, yet most accounts fall short of capturing Chinese travelers' attention.
02
Prohibitive Local Staffing Costs
Hiring a professional social media content editor with visual design capability in China costs at least RMB 10,000 per month for one person — and that's before you find someone who truly understands your industry, your audience, and can operate the account consistently and stably. The true cost of in-house operation is far higher than most realize.
03
Trade Show Costs — and the Aftermath
Exhibitions require substantial investment, but the real challenge begins after the show: keeping newly met buyers engaged. Sending follow-up PDFs by email or WeChat is a passive approach. When buyers actively follow your WeChat account, your regularly updated content continuously rekindles their memory and builds an ongoing connection with your destination. We also help localize your promotional materials so your brand makes a strong, professional impression at every trade show.
04
Platform Registration Barriers
Registering core Chinese content platforms — WeChat Official Account, Xiaohongshu, and others — requires a verified Chinese business entity or a real-name individual identity verification. This presents a genuine obstacle for overseas enterprises entering the China market. Yet these platforms represent your brand's storefront and your destination's image in China — their promotional impact is significant and cannot be ignored.
05
Cultural & Aesthetic Gap
Chinese high-end consumers have entirely different decision journeys, content preferences, and information-seeking behaviors compared to Western travelers. Reusing overseas marketing materials in China rarely works — and poor visual presentation can actively damage a premium brand image.
06

We have already solved all of these challenges for you. From platform registration and daily content operations to trade show representation and cultural localization — our end-to-end partnership removes every barrier between your brand and the Chinese traveler.

Registration Process

WeChat Official Account Setup

Setting up a WeChat Official Account as a foreign entity is complex. We manage the entire process — two clear pathways depending on your situation.

Option 1 · Recommended
You Have a Registered Company in China
Fastest path to full verification and feature access. Full ownership under your Chinese entity.
  1. Document CollectionYou provide business license, legal representative ID, corporate bank account, and certification documents.
  2. Registration & VerificationWe complete account registration and official WeChat verification, set up basic menus and functions.
  3. Permission ConfigurationWe configure account access. Ownership remains with your company.
  4. Official OperationWe deliver daily content, design, community interaction, and monthly data reports.
  5. Ongoing SupportRegular content review, function adjustment, and annual certification renewal.
Option 2
No Chinese Company Yet
Two pathways available — standard overseas Service Account or quick-launch personal account.
  1. Standard: Register Overseas Service AccountWe collect your overseas company documents, contact person ID, and bank info. We submit and complete registration for the overseas corporate Service Account.
  2. Permission SetupWe set up operation permissions. The account belongs to your overseas travel agency.
  3. Temporary: Personal Account Quick LaunchWe register a personal account for immediate content release and fan engagement during the transition.
  4. Migration When ReadyOnce you establish a Chinese company, we migrate all content and followers to your new corporate account.
Note: If you already own a WeChat Official Account but are unsatisfied with its operation or content quality, you only need to grant our team operation access under your guidance. Every login and publishing action is logged with real-name traceability — ensuring full transparency and account security.
